Paneer Ki Sabji

Quick Paneer Curry

Ingredients

  • 350g paneer, cubed
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 1.5 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 1.5 medium tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1.5 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 3/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1.5 tablespoons fresh cream (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Marination and Paneer Preparation

  1. In a bowl, mix 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder, and a pinch of salt with the paneer cubes.
  2. Let the paneer marinate for 10 minutes.
  3. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan and shallow fry the marinated paneer cubes until golden brown on the outside.
  4. Remove the paneer cubes from the pan and set them aside.

Instructions

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in the same pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook until the tomatoes soften and oil separates.
  5. Add water and bring to a boil to form the curry base.
  6. Add the fried paneer cubes, mix gently, and let simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  7. Stir in garam masala and fresh cream (if using). Adjust salt as needed.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti, naan, or steamed rice.

Recipe adapted from Hebbar's Kitchen. Enjoy this quick and easy paneer curry!
