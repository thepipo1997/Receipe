Paneer Ki Sabji

Quick Paneer Curry

Ingredients

  • 350g paneer, cubed
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cream
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Marination and Paneer Preparation

  1. In a bowl, mix 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder, and a pinch of salt with the paneer cubes.
  2. Let the paneer marinate for 10 minutes.
  3. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan and shallow fry the marinated paneer cubes until golden brown on the outside.
  4. Remove the paneer cubes from the pan and set them aside.

Instructions

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in the same pan over medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds and bay leaf, and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  4. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook until the tomatoes soften and oil separates.
  6. Pour in 1/2 cup water and mix well to form a curry base.
  7. Add the fried paneer cubes and mix gently. Cover and simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Stir in garam masala and fresh cream, and adjust salt to taste.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti, naan, or steamed rice.

Recipe adapted from Hebbar's Kitchen. Enjoy this quick and flavorful paneer curry!
